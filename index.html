The regulatory monitoring problem is a math problem

Regulators and public sources publish hundreds of documents every week. Your job is to find the handful that affect your operation — before they affect your operation.

500+
Documents per week
Published across the Federal Register, FDA, FSIS, EPA, OSHA, FTC, and NIST. Most have nothing to do with food and beverage. But the ones that do can change everything.

That means 95% of what you scan is noise. But you can't skip the scanning, because the cost of missing the 5% — a warning letter, a recall, a failed audit — is catastrophic.
